GAME FEATURES

👁️

DIRECTIONAL VISION

Enemies have a directional cone of sight. Slip behind their field of view and stay invisible. Walls block all detection — no cheap hits.

STEALTH
⚠️

3-STAGE ALERT SYSTEM

SAFE → SUSPICIOUS → DETECTED. You get a warning before it's game over. React fast and slip away before the alarm triggers.

DETECTION
🔄

SMART PATROL AI

Guards patrol paths, pause at intersections and randomly pick new routes. Learn their patterns — then exploit them.

Navigate a Pacman-style grid maze. Collect all dots, grab the key, and reach the exit before you're spotted.
